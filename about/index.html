About us
As a London born 5 piece band, Kinoko Curse was formed in the chaos of university. This alt-rock band is known for their powerful live performances that entrance the audience. Their multifaceted music ties grunge undertones with ethereal Cranberries inspired vocals. Kinoko Curse have headlined Camden Assembly and supported at 02 Islington and XOYO.
The band began as a 3 piece consisting of Annabella (Vocals/Bass), James (Vocals/Guitar) and Jacob (Drums). They quickly realised that the sound they were striving towards couldn't be attained with just one guitar. A month after the formation of Kinoko Curse, the band recruited Alex as an additional guitarist. The band would perform and write as a 4 piece for the best part of 7 months.
